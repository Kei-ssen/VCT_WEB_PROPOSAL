VALORANT CHAMPIONS TOUR

Everything You Need to Know

The Valorant Champions Tour (VCT) is a global competitive esports tournament series for the video game Valorant organised by Riot Games, the game's developers. The series runs multiple events throughout each season, culminating in Valorant Champions, the top-level event of the tour..

  • 01

    Regional Leagues

    Featuring teams from specific regions, providing a pathway for aspiring players to compete at the highest level and potentially qualify for international events.

  • 02

    VCT Masters Stage 1

    Premier international Valorant tournament featuring top teams from various regions.

  • 03

    VCT Masters Stage 2

    Second major international Valorant tournament of the year, bringing together top international teams to compete for the championship title.

  • 04

    VCT Champions

    Pinnacle event of the Valorant esports calendar, featuring the world's best teams competing for the ultimate title.
